# Whisperwood v0 → v1: First Iteration

## What Changed

### PostProcessing (VFX: +2 points)
- Added UnrealBloomPass for soft glow on light sources
- Added VignetteShader for cinematic depth
- Added ChromaticAberrationShader for film-like quality
- OutputPass for proper tone mapping

### Collect Effects (VFX: +2 points)
- Particle burst system (24 particles per collect)
- Color-matched particles (mushroom=green, flower=pink, crystal=blue)
- 0.6s lifetime with fade-out

- Random X/Z tilt (±0.15 rad)

- More organic forest feel

- Bird chirps via oscillator
- Layered ambient soundscape
