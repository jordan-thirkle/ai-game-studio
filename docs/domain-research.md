## Important limitation

The initial public search and DNS lookups did not provide authoritative registrar availability or pricing. A domain without DNS records is not proof that it is available. No domain was purchased.

## Pricing/availability action

Before Phase B, check each candidate directly through a registrar that exposes current registration and renewal pricing. Record:

- Availability state.
- First-year price.
- Renewal price.
- Transfer price.
- Privacy/WHOIS inclusion.
- Premium-domain surcharge.
- Taxes and currency.
- Registrar and lookup timestamp.

Do not purchase unilaterally. The strongest naming candidate remains `eigen.studio` if available at a reasonable renewal price, but this document deliberately makes no availability claim.
