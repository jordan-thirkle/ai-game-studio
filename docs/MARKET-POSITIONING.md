# Market Positioning — Eigen AI Studio
**Date:** 2026-07-17
**Cost:** $0.018 (GPT-5.6 Luna via Merge Gateway)

---

## 1. Where Eigen Fits in the Market

Eigen is an AI-native product studio operating between an indie game publisher, SaaS startup, and technical agency. It rapidly turns ideas into polished, deployable products — using AI agents, modern web infrastructure, and reusable systems to build games, developer tools, and subscription software faster and more efficiently than traditional studios.

## 2. What Makes Eigen Unique

- **Multi-product engine:** Eigen can build and launch games, SaaS, AI tools, and client products using a shared technical foundation.
- **AI-native execution:** AI agents accelerate development, content creation, customer support, analytics, and product iteration.
- **Distribution-led building:** Products are designed for specific channels — CrazyGames for games, Product Hunt for SaaS, GitHub for tools — rather than relying only on paid acquisition.

## 3. Pricing

### Game Distribution
- **Free:** Core game at no cost; monetize through ads, sponsorships, or platform revenue share.
- **Pay What You Want:** Optional player contributions, supporter packs, cosmetic upgrades.
- **Recommended:** Keep base game free, test optional purchases after establishing retention.

### SaaS Products
- **Starter — $19/mo:** Individual users, basic limits, core features.
- **Pro — $49/mo:** Higher limits, automation, integrations, priority support.
- **Team — $99/mo:** Multiple users, collaboration, advanced analytics, admin controls.
- Annual billing at ~2 months free.

### Agency / AI Product Development
- **$150–250/hr:** Standard implementation and engineering.
- **$250–350/hr:** AI systems, architecture, complex product development.
- **$350–500/hr:** Strategic, high-urgency, or technically specialized.
- Fixed-scope packages preferred, 50% upfront, milestone-based billing.

## 4. Distribution Channels

- **Games:** CrazyGames first; supplement with itch.io, Poki, social clips, gaming communities.
- **SaaS:** Product Hunt launch, founder-led outreach, SEO landing pages, demos, targeted communities.
- **Tools:** GitHub, Hacker News, Reddit, X, developer newsletters, open-source integrations.

## 5. Six-Month Plan

**Month 1 — Ship Sky Drifter**
- Polish onboarding, controls, performance, mobile support.
- Launch on CrazyGames, collect retention/session-length/monetization data.
- Publish gameplay clips, begin building audience.

**Month 2 — Launch PromptForge**
- MVP with curated prompts, search, categories, user submissions, Stripe payments.
- Launch on Product Hunt and AI communities.
- Validate pricing, activation, repeat usage.

**Months 3–4 — Improve and Monetize**
- Double down on strongest retention/revenue product.
- Add paid tiers, usage limits, referrals, analytics, lifecycle email.
- Package reusable AI/game dev capabilities for agency clients.

**Months 5–6 — Scale Revenue**
- Grow PromptForge through SEO, partnerships, creator acquisition.
- Release one GitHub-based AI tool for inbound demand.
- Pursue higher-value agency contracts, reinvest into best-performing product.
