### Naming Patterns That Work

1. **Greek/Latin roots** — Anthropic, Nous, Adept, Cohorte
2. **Nature forces** — Mistral, DeepMind, Storm
3. **Mathematical/scientific** — Tensor, Lambda, Sigma
4. **Action verbs** — Runway, Replit, Resend
5. **Invented words** — Vercel, Supabase (real-sounding but made up)

### What Doesn't Work

- **"AI" in the name** — too generic, dated already
- **"Labs" suffix** — overused (Google Labs, Meta Labs, etc.)
- **"Corp" or "Inc"** — feels corporate, not creative
- **Made-up gibberish** — Xyzzy, Qwerty (no meaning)
- **Too long** — anything over 3 syllables is hard to remember

## Deep Dive: Eigen

### Etymology
- **German:** eigen = "own", "inherent", "characteristic"
- **Mathematics:** eigenvalue = a scalar associated with a linear transformation that represents the transformation's fundamental nature
- **Physics:** eigenstate = a definite state of a quantum system
- **Computer Science:** eigendecomposition = breaking a matrix into its fundamental components

### Why Eigen Is Perfect for This Studio

**1. Mathematical Precision**
We score games on a 100-point rubric. We measure quality with data. We're not guessing — we're computing the eigenvalue of quality.

**2. Fundamental Nature**
An eigenvalue reveals what something truly IS at its core. Our studio builds products that have inherent quality — not surface-level polish that falls apart under scrutiny.

**3. Uniqueness in AI Space**
No major AI company uses "Eigen." It's fresh. It's distinctive. It's not another "Neural" or "Cognitive" or "Synth."

**4. Product Naming**
- Eigen Studio — the studio itself
- Eigen Score — our scoring system
- Eigen Forge — our game engine
- Eigen OS — our autonomous operating system
- Eigen CLI — our command-line tools

**5. Domain Availability**
- eigen.studio — premium, clean
- eigen.dev — developer-focused
- eigen.ai — if available

### The Story

"Every piece of software has an inherent nature — its eigen. Some software is fundamentally good. Some is fundamentally broken. We build software that is fundamentally, inherently excellent. Not because we add polish. Because the quality is intrinsic to how we build it."

## Alternative Deep Dives

### Axiom
- A statement accepted as true without proof
- Strength: Confidence, self-evidence
- Weakness: Overused in math/logic context (Axiom Zen, etc.)

### Cortex
- The brain's outer layer — where thinking happens
- Strength: Direct brain metaphor
- Weakness: Generic — many companies use it (Cortex Brain, Cortex AI)

### Helix
- DNA double helix — the blueprint of life
- Strength: Evolutionary, organic, building blocks
- Weakness: Overused (Helix Energy, Helix Water)

### Nexus
- A central connection point
- Strength: Hub, center, connection
- Weakness: Very common — Nexus mods, Nexus phones, etc.

### Synapse
- Connection between neurons
- Strength: Brain chemistry, signal processing
- Weakness: Medical connotation, less premium

### Crucible
- Container for melting at high heat to purify
- Strength: Transformation, purification, intensity
- Weakness: Dark, aggressive connotation
