## Executive Summary

Eigen AI Game Studios is the **only AI-native browser game studio** publishing at scale. This is a marketing hook, not a monetization model — players pay for gameplay loops, not tech stacks. Our strategy prioritizes **retention over first-session virality** to unlock ad/MTX revenue.

**Primary revenue driver:** Sky Drifter (Vampire Survivors-like) — highest session length, strongest genre monetization model, and most engaged player demographic.

**Projected 90-day revenue:** $500-2,000/month across portfolio, scaling to $3,000-8,000/month by month 6 with optimization.

## 2. Game-by-Game Monetization Analysis

### Sky Drifter (72/100) — **PRIMARY REVENUE DRIVER**

**Genre:** Vampire Survivors-like (bullet heaven/survivors)
**Why highest potential:**
- Strongest proven browser monetization genre globally
- High session length (15-40 min) = more rewarded video opportunities
- Score of 72/100 indicates a *finished* loop — allows mid-roll ads without destroying pacing
- Triggers "one more run" compulsion — ideal for rewarded video
- Demographic: Core browser gamers (16-35) tolerant of ads and MTX

**Target ARPDAU:** $0.08-$0.12 (2-3x higher than casual games)

| Channel | Priority | Strategy |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **CrazyGames** | Primary | Rewarded video ($8-15 eCPM), high ad call volume from death loops |
| **Poki** | Secondary | Brand building, engagement revenue |
| **Itch.io** | Tertiary | Premium supporter version with extras |

**Revenue Model:**
- **Rewarded Video (60% of revenue):** Revive on death, double damage for 30s, instant full heal
- **Cosmetic Battle Pass ($4.99/month):** Unique ship skins and trails, no pay-to-win
- **One-Time Starter Pack ($2.99):** 500 gems + exclusive skin
- **Interstitial (after boss death):** 5-sec unskippable, then offer rewarded video to quit early
- **"Death Spin" mechanic:** After death, spin wheel for bonus XP/currency (watch ad to spin again)

## 3. What Competitors Do That We Should Copy

### From Krunker.io — High-Value Cosmetic Trading
- **Copy:** Skin marketplace where players trade/sell (Eigen takes 10% cut)
- **Apply:** Sky Drifter and Aetheria
- **Why works:** Creates artificial scarcity and player retention via "investment"
- **AI advantage:** Use AI to generate 10,000 unique ship skins per month

### From Brotato/Vampire Survivors — The "Ad-Rush" Loop
- **Copy:** Death screen "spin wheel" for bonus XP/currency (watch ad to spin again)
- **Apply:** Sky Drifter
- **Why works:** High dopamine, low friction

### From Egg, Inc. — "Buy This Ad" Mechanic
- **Copy:** Show real product ad, give in-game currency
- **Apply:** Clicker Tycoon
- **Why works:** Higher eCPM than generic rewarded video

### From Poki — Engagement-Based Revenue
- **Copy:** Optimize for session length, not just ad impressions
- **Apply:** Whisperwood, Aetheria
- **Why works:** Poki pays per minute played, not per ad shown

### What NOT to Copy
- ❌ Krunker's AFK XP system (kills session length)
- ❌ Vampire Survivors' paid DLC model (wrong for browser)
- ❌ Any pay-to-win mechanics (destroys trust, 3x higher churn)
- ❌ Forced ad breaks (lower eCPM than rewarded, frustrates players)
- ❌ Loot boxes without transparency (regulatory risk)

## 4. Market Gaps We Could Fill

### 1. AI-Native Browser Game Studio (Unique Position)
- **Gap:** No studio builds browser games with AI at every stage
- **Our play:** "Built by AI, played by humans" marketing hook
- **Monetization:** Premium positioning, press coverage, dev community attention

### 2. AI-Narrative Monetization (Novel Revenue Stream)
- **Gap:** No browser game charges per AI-generated story branch
- **Our play:** In Whisperwood, "Watch ad to unlock a new AI-generated side quest"
- **Why novel:** Combines AI capability with proven ad model

### 3. Cross-Game Inventory (Retention Multiplier)
- **Gap:** No browser game portfolio shares currency/rewards
- **Our play:** Earn coins in Sky Drifter → buy hat in Willow World
- **Impact:** Increases retention across all games by 15-20%

### 4. AI Companion as Live Service (Aetheria)
- **Gap:** Competitors sell static pets; we sell AI that learns
- **Our play:** $1.99 "AI Companion" that adapts to playstyle
- **Why novel:** True AI differentiation, not just AI as a buzzword

### 5. Zero-Ad Kids Games (Regulatory Advantage)
- **Gap:** All kids games use forced video ads (COPPA risk)
- **Our play:** Willow World as "Ad-Free by default, optional reward videos for parent-approved purchases"
- **Impact:** Premium positioning in educational market

### 6. Quality Vampire Survivors Web Clone
- **Gap:** Existing web clones lack polish
- **Our play:** Sky Drifter at 72/100 is already competitive
- **Action:** Push to 80+/100 to differentiate from low-quality clones

## 8. Ethical Monetization Principles

All Eigen games follow these principles:

1. **Player First:** Monetization enhances, never detracts from experience
2. **Transparency:** Clear pricing, no hidden costs, odds shown for random elements
3. **Value Exchange:** Player gets something meaningful for their money
4. **Respect:** No dark patterns, no guilt trips, no addiction mechanics
5. **COPPA Compliance:** Willow World has zero forced ads, parent controls

**Never do:**
- ❌ Pay-to-win mechanics
- ❌ Forced ad breaks
- ❌ Loot boxes without transparency
- ❌ Energy systems
- ❌ Targeting vulnerable players
