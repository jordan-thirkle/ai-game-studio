# Studio Naming Research

## The Criteria

A great AI studio name must be:
1. **Memorable** — one word, easy to say, sticks in your head
2. **Meaningful** — has depth, tells a story when you look it up
3. **Unique** — not another "AI Labs" or "Neural Corp"
4. **Timeless** — won't feel dated in 2030
5. **Versatile** — works as a prefix for products (Eigen Score, Eigen Forge, Eigen OS)
6. **Grounded** — real word with real meaning, not made up

## What Works in AI (2026)

The best AI companies don't use "AI" in their name:
- **Anthropic** — human-like (Greek: anthropos)
- **OpenAI** — exception (but they own the category)
- **Nous Research** — nous = Greek for "mind/intellect"
- **DeepMind** — depth of intelligence
- **Mistral** — a wind, speed + power
- **Cohorte** — group, collective intelligence
- **Adept** — skilled, capable

Pattern: **Real words with intellectual/scientific roots.**

## Name Candidates

### Tier 1: The One

**Eigen**
- German: "own", "inherent", "characteristic"
- Math: eigenvalues, eigenvectors — the fundamental properties of a system
- Meaning: What something truly is at its core. The intrinsic nature.
- Why it fits: We build things that have their own identity. Each game, each product has an "eigen" — a fundamental character that makes it what it is. The scoring system measures the eigen of quality.
- Products: Eigen Studio, Eigen Score, Eigen Forge, Eigen OS
- Domain: eigen.studio (available?)
- Feels: Mathematical, precise, European, premium

### Tier 2: Strong Contenders

**Axiom**
- A self-evident truth. Something so obviously correct it needs no proof.
- Why it fits: Our products should be axiomatically good. The quality speaks for itself.
- Products: Axiom Games, Axiom Tools, Axiom AI
- Feels: Confident, mathematical, clean

**Cortex**
- The outer layer of the brain where thinking happens
- Why it fits: We're the thinking layer on top of code. The intelligence that makes software intelligent.
- Products: Cortex Studio, Cortex Engine, Cortex Score
- Feels: Brain-like, scientific, powerful

**Helix**
- The DNA double helix — the blueprint of life
- Why it fits: We write the DNA of software. Each product is a new strand.
- Products: Helix Games, Helix Studio, Helix Lab
- Feels: Organic, scientific, evolutionary

**Prism**
- Splits light into its component colors
- Why it fits: We take complex problems and break them into beautiful, understandable parts. Also: the creative director's name.
- Products: Prism Studio, Prism Engine, Prism AI
- Feels: Visual, creative, precise

### Tier 3: Alternative Directions

**Synapse**
- The connection between neurons where signals fire
- Why it fits: We connect ideas, code, and creativity

**Nexus**
- A central connection point
- Why it fits: We're the hub where AI meets software

**Vortex**
- A spinning force that pulls everything in
- Why it fits: Our flywheel pulls everything into one focused point

**Forge**
- Where raw materials become tools
- Why it fits: We forge software from AI. But "Forge Labs" is taken by Microsoft.

**Crucible**
- A container for melting things at high heat to purify them
- Why it fits: We put code through the fire to make it pure

## Recommendation

**Eigen** is the name.

It's one word. It's mathematical. It means "inherent nature" — the fundamental quality of something. It's unique in the AI space. It works as a prefix for every product. It sounds like a real company, not a startup trying too hard.

The studio is **Eigen**. We build things that have their own inherent quality. We don't make generic software. We make software that is fundamentally, inherently good.
